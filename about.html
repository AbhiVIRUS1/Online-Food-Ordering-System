About us

Nomnom is an online shopping/marketing place for all your tummy needs. We are currently operational in Brahmapur, Odisha only. As our headquarters is in Brahmapur, we promise quicker delivery of genuine products.

We deal with your favorite food outlets in one online platform delivered in Brahmapur. Our main aim is being the comfort of our customers so we are making it easy for them to order all their yummy tummy needs and deliver it to them within hours to spread happiness and let them celebrate more time with their loved ones from the heavenly comfort called home.

We believes in quality because that is how we want to build our customer base. Along with great quality, we want to give the customers an experience of happiness and hence we are giving them discounts without compromising the quality of the product. Discounts may vary from seller to seller.
